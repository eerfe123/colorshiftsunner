# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 18, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Serpent Evolution ("the App," "we," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile game. By using Serpent Evolution, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Serpent Evolution does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or submit any personally identifiable information (PII) to play. We do not collect, store, or have access to:

- Your name, email address, or phone number
- Your precise or coarse device location
- Your contacts, calendar, or photos
- Your payment or credit card information (all purchases are handled by Google Play)
- Any biometric or health-related data

---

## 2. Game Data Stored On Your Device

All of your game progress data is stored locally on your device using Android's built-in DataStore storage. This data never leaves your device and is never uploaded to any server. The data we store locally includes:

- High scores, survival times, and total games played
- Coins earned and spent, plus your purchase history for ad removal and skins
- Your selected snake skin and list of unlocked skins
- Evolution stage progress and experience points
- Achievements earned and challenges completed
- Daily reward streak and claims history
- Settings preferences (music, sound effects, vibration, control mode, graphics quality)

**This data is yours.** If you uninstall the App or clear your app data through Android Settings, this data will be permanently deleted.

---

## 3. Advertising & AdMob

### What ads do we show?

Serpent Evolution displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising network. The ad types shown are:

- **Banner ads** — Small banners displayed at the bottom of non-gameplay screens (Main Menu, Shop, Rewards, and Game Over screen).
- **Interstitial ads** — Full-screen ads shown occasionally after every few game-over sessions, only when you tap Restart or Main Menu. They never appear during gameplay or before you see your score.
- **Rewarded ads** — Optional video ads you choose to watch in exchange for in-game benefits (reviving after death, doubling your coins, or opening a bonus chest). Rewarded ads are always player-initiated.

### What data does AdMob collect?

Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information from your device to serve ads, including:

- Device identifiers (such as your Android Advertising ID)
- IP address, which may provide coarse location information
- Device type, operating system version, and mobile network information
- Ad interaction data (which ads you see and tap)

### How to control ad personalization

You can reset your advertising ID, opt out of interest-based advertising, or delete your advertising ID entirely through your device settings under **Settings → Google → Ads**. You can also remove all non-optional ads permanently by purchasing the Remove Ads option in the in-game shop.

---

## 4. Analytics & Usage Data

Serpent Evolution may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to help us understand how players interact with the game and to improve the experience. This data includes:

- Game session events: game starts, game overs, scores, survival times, and evolution stages reached
- Feature usage: which skins are purchased, which challenges are completed, which power-ups are collected
- Ad interactions: when rewarded ads are watched and interstitial ads are shown
- Purchase events: when in-app purchases are completed

### What this data does NOT include:

No personal identifiers, no device IDs, no IP addresses, no location data. All analytics data is stripped of any information that could identify an individual player. This data is aggregated across all players and used solely to improve gameplay balance and fix issues.

---

## 5. In-App Purchases

Serpent Evolution offers optional in-app purchases through the Google Play Store, including:

- **Remove Ads** — Permanently removes all banner and interstitial ads
- **Coin Packs** — Buy in-game coins used for unlocking skins and other cosmetic items

All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information — your credit card, billing address, and transaction details are managed exclusively by Google. In-app purchases are subject to Google Play's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Your purchase history (such as whether you own Remove Ads or which coin packs you have bought) is stored locally on your device to unlock the corresponding features and content in the game.

## 8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since all game data is stored locally on your device and we do not operate any backend servers, the security of your game data relies on Android's built-in application sandbox and storage security. We do not transmit your game data over the internet.

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data stored on your device.

## 9. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following choices regarding your data:

- **Opt out of ad personalization:** Go to Settings → Google → Ads on your Android device and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or delete your Advertising ID.
- **Remove all non-optional ads:** Purchase the Remove Ads option in the in-game shop to permanently disable banner and interstitial ads.
- **Delete all game data:** Uninstall the app or go to Settings → Apps → Serpent Evolution → Storage → Clear Data. This will permanently erase all local game data.
- **Restore purchases:** If you reinstall the app, use the Restore Purchases button in Settings to recover your paid items through Google Play.
- **Control in-app purchases:** You can require authentication for purchases through Google Play Settings.

Depending on your jurisdiction (such as the GDPR in the European Economic Area or the CCPA in California), you may have additional rights to access, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. Since we do not collect personal data, these rights are typically exercised through the third-party services listed in Section 6.
