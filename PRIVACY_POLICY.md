## Summary

Color Shift Runner is a hyper-casual runner game. **We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.** All game data (scores, coins, skins, settings) is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server.

---

## What Data We Collect

### Data Stored On-Device Only (Not Collected by Us)

| Data Category | Examples | Purpose |
|---------------|----------|---------|
| Game progress | High score, coins earned, distance records | Enable gameplay persistence across app sessions |
| Inventory | Unlocked skins, owned cosmetics, battle pass tiers | Remember what you've earned |
| Preferences | Sound on/off, music on/off, haptics on/off | Remember your settings |
| Progression | Player level, XP, daily streak, mission progress | Track your in-game advancement |

**This data never leaves your device.** It is stored in Android's built-in app storage (SharedPreferences) and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

### Data Collected by Third-Party Services

#### Google AdMob (Advertising)

We display ads through Google AdMob, a Google Play certified ad network. AdMob may collect:

- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Coarse location (derived from IP)
- App interaction data (for ad performance measurement)

**For users in the EEA and UK:** AdMob serves only non-personalized ads by default, as required by GDPR.  
**For child users:** AdMob serves only child-appropriate ads, as required by Google Play Families Policy and COPPA.

You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads at any time through:
- **Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**

### Data We DO NOT Collect

- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Account credentials or passwords
- Photos, videos, or files
- Precise location (GPS)
- Contacts, calendar, or call logs
- Health, fitness, or biometric data
- Microphone or camera recordings
- Any user-generated content

## Children's Privacy

Color Shift Runner is designed to be appropriate for all ages. We comply with:

- **COPPA (US Children's Online Privacy Protection Act):** We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All data is stored locally on-device with no server transmission.
- **GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation):** We serve non-personalized ads by default in the EEA/UK. No personal data is collected or processed.
- **Google Play Families Policy:** Ads are served exclusively through Google AdMob, a Google Play certified ad network. All ad content is filtered to be appropriate for children.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, contact us immediately.

## Advertising

### What Ads Appear
- **Rewarded Ads:** Shown when you choose to continue after crashing, double your coins, or claim bonus rewards.
- **Interstitial Ads:** Shown after every 3–4 failed runs (never during active gameplay).

### Ad Network
All advertising is delivered through **Google AdMob**, which is listed in the [Google Play certified ad networks program](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9283445).

### Opting Out
To opt out of interest-based advertising on your Android device:
1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap **Google → Ads**
3. Enable **Opt out of Ads Personalization**
